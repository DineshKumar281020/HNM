OUR COMPANY

Developing Our People, Growing Our Business

HNM was established in the year 1988 to cater to the pump, valve and other General Engineering Industries located abundantly across Coimbatore, a well recognized and respected Industrial destination in India.

Since 1988, HNM has developed personnel, facilities, materials, and processes required to recognize and meet its valuable customer’s rubber needs.

Our ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing facility, occupying 4000square feet of floor space, backed by 30 skilled and committed workforce dedicated to giving their best to the industry requirements.

With a unique combination of Knowledge, experience, State of –the-art equipments and quality manufacturing systems, HNM manufacture wide range of Rubber Products and Rubber to metal bonded products exactly as per the requirements of its clients.

For manufacturing companies, content marketing (or inbound marketing) is one of the strongest strategies to not only attract customers but also to keep the interest of existing customers. It not only boosts your SEO and helps your industrial company be found online, but it also educates your prospects so they can make informed decisions about their purchases. The key to industrial content marketing is creating content for search intent. With 70% of B2B buyers completing their research online before even reaching out to a potential vendor, it’s imperative that you’re creating the right content for your buyers at the right time. A buyer has different motivations at each stage of their buying process and is therefore looking for different types of resources. If you’re developing your content with the intention of helping and educating your prospects vs. selling them on your business, you’re already halfway there to nailing your industrial content marketing strategy!

Industrial companies create white papers that delve into a specific topic or problem common to many in their target audience. The white paper provides in-depth analysis, research, and insights.
